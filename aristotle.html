THE ARISTOCRATIC TEAM

As the President of SERVICOM Community Development Service (CDS), I am proud to highlight the following achievements that myself and my team were able to implement in the space of 7 months in office.

These achievements demonstrate our dedication to community development, member empowerment, and social responsibility. As President, I am proud of our collective efforts and look forward to continued growth and impact.

Key Achievements

Customized Clearance Stamp

Replaced signatures with a more efficient and secure engraved clearance stamp on biometric days, streamlining our processes and enhancing authenticity.

Philanthropic Initiatives

Distributed clothing, food, and packages to Abiye Orphanage Home, demonstrating our commitment to community service.

Social Events

Organized the first-ever pool party and hosted a fun trip to Erinjesa Waterfall, enhancing camaraderie and team spirit.

Sensitization Programs

Conducted sensitization on sex education and adolescent development, and organized a symposium on career building and leadership roles.

Tech Symposium

Brought together dynamic minds in a transformative symposium focused on career development, digital skills, and future-ready leadership—equipping members with actionable insights and inspiration.

Branding and Identity

Designed and distributed engraved SERVICOM wristbands, introduced a new banner, documentation books, and attendance book.

Digital Presence

Created Instagram and TikTok accounts engaging over 2,000 users and developed an official website for global recognition.
