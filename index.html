About SERVICOM CDS

SERVICOM CDS Group is one of the Community Development Service (CDS) groups under the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) in Osogbo, Osun State. Our primary objective is to promote service excellence and ensure customer satisfaction in all NYSC activities.

We organize various programs and initiatives aimed at improving service delivery, creating awareness about SERVICOM principles, and fostering a culture of excellence among corps members and the host community.

Our activities include sensitization programs, workshops, community service projects, and collaborations with government agencies to improve public service delivery.

Our Services

Service Delivery Monitoring

We monitor and evaluate service delivery in NYSC and partner organizations to ensure compliance with SERVICOM standards.

Customer Satisfaction Surveys

We conduct surveys to assess customer satisfaction with NYSC services and provide recommendations for improvement.

Sensitization Programs

We organize workshops and awareness campaigns to educate corps members and the public about SERVICOM principles.
