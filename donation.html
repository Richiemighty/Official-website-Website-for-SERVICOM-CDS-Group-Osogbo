SERVICOM CDS Donates Educational Materials

Supporting NYSC Foundation Nursery & Primary School, Osogbo

Event Overview

On July 16, 2025, the SERVICOM Community Development Service (CDS) group made a generous donation of educational materials to the NYSC Foundation Nursery and Primary School, Omo West, Osogbo, Osun State. This impactful event was attended by NYSC officials, school staff, and dedicated Corps members, all united in their commitment to improving education for young learners.

"This donation represents our commitment to community development and the future of education in Osogbo. We're proud to support this school established by a Corps member in 1993."

- Taiwo Emmanuel, SERVICOM CDS President

Event Highlights

Notable Attendees

  • NYSC Osun State Coordinator, Mr. Agbor Ndoma Obim, represented by Mr. Aleshinloye Adeniyi, Assistant Director of Skills Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development
  • LGI Osogbo LGA, Mrs. Jimoh Tawa
  • Assistant Director for CDS, Mrs Taiwo Fatima
  • Mr. Kazeem Aderibigbe, SERVICOM Desk Officer

Donated Materials

  • Three white marker boards
  • Three board cleaners
  • Two packs of markers

Mr. Aleshinloye Adeniyi, receiving the materials on behalf of the school, lauded the CDS group's endeavor, noting that the materials would enhance the school's learning environment. He encouraged the Corps members to participate in SAED to acquire valuable skills for after their service year.
